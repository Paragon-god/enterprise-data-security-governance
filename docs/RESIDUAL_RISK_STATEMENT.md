# Residual Risk Statement

## Decision

The prototype is suitable for portfolio demonstration and controlled improvement planning.

Production deployment is not approved.

## Overall residual risk

Overall residual risk is Medium.

## Basis for the decision

The project demonstrates credible controls for:

* data type and schema validation;
* controlled parsing;
* null and record count reconciliation;
* anomaly analysis;
* secret separation;
* TLS protected database connections;
* model metric disclosure;
* human oversight; and
* sanitised evidence publication.

Material gaps remain in:

* production role enforcement;
* private database connectivity;
* authenticated applications;
* central logging and alerting;
* tested recovery objectives;
* approved retention automation;
* formal vendor assurance;
* incident response exercises; and
* independent model validation.

## Risk acceptance

A real organisation would require formal acceptance by accountable data, security, privacy, model, and business owners.

This document is a portfolio governance conclusion and not an operational approval.
